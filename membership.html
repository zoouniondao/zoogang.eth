About ZooGangGenesis Assets

ZooGangGenesis assets are membership tokens that grant access to exclusive virtual and physical community spaces and places. These assets are designed to be a symbol of belonging and identity for members of the ZooGangGenesis community.

Membership

To become a member of the ZooGangGenesis community, you must acquire a ZooGangGenesis asset. These assets are available for purchase on our website or through authorized resellers. Once you have acquired an asset, you will gain access to our virtual community spaces, such as our Discord server and online forums, as well as physical community spaces, such as our events and meetups.

Community

By joining our community, you will have access to a wealth of knowledge and resources, as well as the opportunity to connect and collaborate with like-minded individuals.
