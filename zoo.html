Sandbox Game - Virtual Zoo Experience

Join us in building one of the first virtual zoo experiences within an open public metaverse with Sandbox Game.

About Sandbox Game

Sandbox Game is a voxel-based multiplayer online game that allows players to create and share their own virtual worlds. The game is built on the Ethereum blockchain, which enables the creation and exchange of in-game assets and tokens. Sandbox Game has a thriving community of players, developers, and creators who are constantly building and exploring new worlds.

Virtual Zoo Experience

We are excited to announce our plan to build one of the first virtual zoo experiences within an open public metaverse using Sandbox Game. Our goal is to create an immersive and educational experience for visitors that showcases a variety of animal species from around the world. By leveraging the power of blockchain technology, we can offer unique and valuable experiences to our visitors, such as the ability to interact with and even own virtual representations of the animals.

Game Maker Fund Application

We have submitted an application to the Game Maker Fund in order to secure funding for our project. The Game Maker Fund is a grant program that supports independent game developers and projects that are built on the Ethereum blockchain. We believe that our virtual zoo experience aligns with the mission of the Game Maker Fund and has the potential to be a game-changing project in the world of blockchain gaming.
